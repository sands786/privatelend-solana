How It Works

Three Steps. Total Privacy.

1

Supply Collateral — Encrypted

Your collateral amount is encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM before it touches Solana. The chain stores only a ciphertext — bots see nothing.

2

Borrow — MXE Computes in Secret

LTV ratio, health factor and interest rate are computed inside Arcium MXE. You see the results. The protocol verifies via ZK proof. Bots see zero.

3

Liquidation — Mathematically Hidden

Liquidation only fires when MXE issues a ZK proof that health < 1.0. The trigger price is never exposed. Front-running is mathematically impossible.
